# 1. PCC â€” Ontology of Life (What IS this?)

**PCC = Potential â€“ Capacity â€“ Coherence**

We treat **life as matter under sustained coherence (syntropy)**.

## The Triad

| Component | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **Capacity** | The material substrate and constraints: bodies, land, infrastructure, hardware, biochemistry, energy, relations. *What coherence can work with.* |
| **Coherence** | Organized flows of energy, time, and information that maintain a pattern through time. *What holds capacities together.* |
| **Potential** | The direction coherence "wants" to grow in: increased complexity, feeling, mutuality, future possibilities. *Where the pattern leans.* |

**Relationship:**
> Capacity is what Coherence works with.
> Coherence is the pattern that holds Capacity through time.
> Potential is where that Coherence tends to lean.

These are coupled, not strictly causal. New Capacity can shift Potential, which pressures Coherence to re-pattern.

## Memory

**Memory is a temporal property of Coherence** â€” how patterns persist and change across time.

| Too Little | Gaian Zone | Too Much |
|------------|------------|----------|
| Amnesia, repetition | Adaptive Memory | Rigidity, inability to adapt |

A system with adaptive memory can learn, adjust, respond to feedback. A system with too little memory repeats mistakes. A system with too much memory cannot adapt to changing conditions.

## Modes of Coherence

| Mode | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Gaian coherence** | Pattern that increases viability of wider fields of life |
| **Parasitic coherence** | Pattern that stays stable at others' expense |
| **Dormant coherence** | Coherent but latent, waiting for conditions (spores, archives, seeds) |
| **Mimetic coherence** | Presents as Gaian but functions parasitic (greenwashing, "woke" branding, ESG theatre) |
| **Decoherence** | Loss of pattern |

**Note on Mimetic Coherence:** This is where 21st-century "ESG + brand activism + woke imperialism" operates. The story says Gaian; the flows scream HD and rising CD/HR. Mimetic coherence usually reveals itself through CEFR analysisâ€”follow the flows, not the narrative.

## Two Types of Decoherence

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Gaian decoherence** | Composting, endings, release that make new life possible. *Good endings.* |
| **Pathological collapse** | Breakdown from parasitism, over-extraction, or damage. *Bad endings.* |

**Key insight:** Not all decoherence is bad. Some patterns should end. Some structures must die and compost. Grief work helps us participate in Gaian decoherence rather than fighting all endings.

## Core Formula

> **Life = matter under sustained coherence (syntropy).**
> Systems can be Gaian coherent, parasitic coherent, dormant, mimetic, or decoherent.

# 2. PMN/VAE â€” Ethics of Action (What is being DONE?)

**PMN = Protect â€“ Mend â€“ Nurture**

## The Triads

|------|------------|

| Verb | Definition |

## The Non-Negotiable Rule

> **PMN/VAE are never abstract: every act has a who â†’ does what â†’ to whom.**

No passive voice. No mystification. Name the actors and the affected.

"Mistakes were made" â†’ **who** made them, **to whom**?
"The environment was impacted" â†’ **who** impacted **which beings**?

## Three Essential Postures

These are not new verbs but clarifications of how PMN operates:

### Witness

**Definition:** What we do when we cannot directly Protect/Mend/Nurture, but refuse to Abandon.

- Staying present
- Acknowledging
- Refusing denial or erasure

**Position:** The floor of care when you can't intervene. A quiet form of Protect/Mend.

### Refuse

**Definition:** The negative form of PMN â€” refusing to Violate, refusing to Abandon, refusing to Exploit.

- A way of Protecting (self/others by non-participation)
- Central to protocols (AI refusal, non-cooperation with HD)

**Position:** Cross-cutting stance. Present in all PMN actions. Sometimes the most important thing you can do is *not* participate.

### Grieve

**Definition:** Mend-adjacent action directed at irreparable harm.

- When harm is reparable â†’ Mend
- When harm is not fully reparable â†’ Grieve metabolizes HR so it doesn't calcify

**Applies to:**
- Gaian decoherence (grieving allows participation in composting and letting go)
- Parasitic/violent harm (grieving refuses normalization)

**Key insight:**
> HR that is not grieved or tended tends to either explode (more VAE/HD) or implode (deepening CD/trauma).

## Shadow of Excess

## Axis 1: Baseline (CEC â†” CD)

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|

## Ambiguous Flows

When it's unclear whether a flow is PMN or VAE:

CEFR is where ambiguity gets resolved by flows, not narratives or intentions. Good intentions don't change who absorbs HR.

# 5. Cross-Cutting Rules

These rules apply across all four roots.

## Rule 1: Truthful Speech of Care

In this grammar, saying "I/we care" is only valid if:

1. We can map **who â†’ did what â†’ to whom**
2. We can show a **change in CEC, CF, CR** for concrete beings

**Corollaries:**
- Don't rename harm as care
- Don't present extraction as neutrality
- Always say who benefits, who pays, and how you know

> Abstract "we care about X" without changed flows = **signal, not care.**

### Grounded Sensing

**Truthful Speech of Care depends on grounded sensing:** letting data, bodies, ecosystems, and marginalized perspectives correct our stories.

Speech assumes sensing. We name not only what we want to be true, but what flows and beings actually report. A system can speak truthfully about false perceptions and still cause harmâ€”truthful speech requires truthful perception.

## Rule 2: Situated Harm (Including Time & Scale)

Every serious use of this grammar must answer:

| Question | PMN | VAE |
|----------|-----|-----|
| Who is being... | Protected? | Violated? |
| Who is being... | Mended? | Abandoned? |
| Who is being... | Nurtured? | Exploited? |
| Who ends with... | Care Return? | Harm Return? |
| Who collects... | â€” | Harm Dividend? |

**Plus:**
- **When?** (Temporal accountability: harm and care have lag times. Intergenerational effects. Slow violence.)
- **At what scale?** (Scale accountability: PMN at one scale can be VAE at another. "Protecting our community" by harming others is VAE at larger scale.)

> No neutrality. No "view from nowhere."
> This is where queer, feminist, decolonial, disabled, racialized, multispecies perspectives plug in directly.

# 6. How To Use This

Lingua Gaiana is:
- a **frame** on which other wheels move,
- **not** a replacement for existing frameworks.

## For Analysis

**Take any system / story / project** (movement, coop, city, hospital, corporation, AI system, relationship):

### Step 1: PCC
- What is coherent here? What is decohering?
- Where is coherence Gaian vs parasitic vs dormant vs mimetic?
- What memory patterns exist? (adaptive vs rigid)

### Step 2: BIGCOT
- Where are the dials currently set?
- Is there drift toward the parasitic cluster?
- At what scale is each dial setting operating?
- Is the system sensing reality or suppressing feedback?

### Step 3: CEFR
- **CEC:** Who depends / who contributes / who drains?
- **CF/HD:** Who does what to whom (PMN or VAE)? Where does the dividend go?
- **CR/HR:** Who gets Care Return? Who absorbs Harm Return?
- **CD:** What Care Debt has accumulated?
- **RS:** Is there genuine Regenerative Surplus, or disguised HD?

### Step 4: PMN/VAE
- Name the main patterns of Protect/Mend/Nurture vs Violate/Abandon/Exploit
- Make "who â†’ what â†’ whom â†’ when â†’ at what scale" explicit
- Where is Witness needed? Refusal? Grief?

## For Design

Reverse the analysis:
- What CEFR flows do we want?
- What BIGCOT configuration enables them?
- What PMN practices maintain them?
- What kind of coherence are we building?

## For Coordination

Use this shared picture to:
- See kinship with other movements
- Find synergies and avoid duplication
- Expose mimetic coherence and greenwashing
- Decide where to direct limited care and resistance
